# Code of Conduct

Raven follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and focused on
the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good faith while asking for evidence.
- Keep discussions technical and actionable.
- Respect maintainers' time and review scope.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
moderation action when behavior disrupts the project or community.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the
project's GitHub organization channels.
